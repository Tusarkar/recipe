Kitchen tips

Small habits that make the codex recipes taste even better.

  • Read once, then cook. Skim the whole recipe before you heat the pan — timing matters.
  • Salt in layers. Season as you go; you can always add, rarely subtract.
  • Rest meat & dough. A few minutes off the heat saves juices and texture.
  • Taste at the end. Acid, salt, and fat balance each other — adjust before plating.

Privacy policy

Copper Codex runs entirely in your browser. Favorites are saved with local storage on your device only — we don’t run a backend that receives your recipe picks. The contact form is a demo: nothing is uploaded until you wire it to a mail or form service.
